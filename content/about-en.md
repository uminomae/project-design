# Project Design

I have experienced what I want to call Love-Driven Development many times.

Every project has "Doing" and "Being."
PMBOK manages schedule, cost, and quality,
but most of what actually happens in a project isn't covered there.

The moment something shifts in dialogue.
The stretch of time spent holding an unanswerable question.
The turning point when emotions become signals, not noise.

This site attempts to articulate that structure.
Emotional processing, Kesson-Driven Thinking, intersubjectivity,
the Five Stages of Creation —
starting from lived experience, working toward theory.

Not yet complete. Thinking while writing.
